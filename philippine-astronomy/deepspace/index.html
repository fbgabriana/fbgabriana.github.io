Objects in Deep Space

This section will contain articles about deep sky objects, including star clusters, nebulas, galaxies, and galaxy clusters.

Aside

A nebula is a giant cloud of dust and gas in space where new stars are usually born.

A star cluster is large groups of stars held together by gravity, usually born from the same nebula.

A galaxy is a huge collection of gas, dust, and billions of stars and star clusters, all held together by gravity.
